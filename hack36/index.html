What is Hack36 ?

Hack36 is the annual hackathon hosted by the Programming Club of IIT Mandi.

Learn more

Objective

Hack36 plans to build an environment of collaborative growth amongst all the participants. Hack36 is open to everyone with a crazy idea and a will to implement it into a working prototype. Hack36 will try their level best to make sure that all candidates get a fair chance to put-forth their ideas and showcase their experience and talent. FurtherMore, we have introduced various amazing prizes to the lucrative participants. See our Code of Conduct Here.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this hackathon an online event or offline?
    Hack36 is offline event and as a participant you need to be studying in IIT Mandi, that's all!!
  • Do this hackathon has any participation fees for students?
    No. The hackathon is free of cost for any student from any college all over India.
  • What are all the restrictions on Team formations for the event?
    A team can have at most 4 members and minimum of 2 members.
  • What should I do if I do not have any team yet?
    Just fill out the application form and according to skills you have filled out, we will match you with the best team possible during Pre-Hackathon meeting.
  • What are the Perks and Benefits of Participating in this Hackathon?
    Hackathon Provide hands on experience with the experts from different field working on the Projects like Web-App, AI, Machine Learning, etc. This will help to give a head start if any one want to make project on the field. Along with that the participant will be provided with cool swags, stickers and refreshments to keep them up.
  • I have a question related to hackathon which is not listed above. What should I do?
    Feel free to reach us out. Just drop us a mail at pc@iitmandi.ac.in
    You can also contact one of our organizers
